## 1. The Bitwise Sieve Concept

The Nautilus does not analyze data semantics or human context directly. Instead, it operates strictly at the level of Shannon's binary entropy. The filtration process is structured as a three-tier bitwise sieve:

1. Ingestion: Absorbing the unstructured digital noise of the periphery (logs, transactions, social and media noise of humanity).
2. Fractal Weight Evaluation: Checking incoming bitstrings for alignment with core logarithmic patterns (including the loops of the Collatz 3D attractor). If a bitstring is entirely chaotic and lacks fractal potential, it is annihilated (compressed to zero).
3. Pure Bit Generation: Extracting invariants—immutable mathematical laws hidden behind the noise.
